How Mock Market uses Ripplesk to support a growing trader community

Mock Market serves a user base focused on learning, practicing, and competing in a simulated trading environment — Ripplesk gives them a unified, scalable support structure without the overhead of complex infrastructure.

Industry
Fintech / Trading Simulation
Use
Community support, AI triage, omnichannel
Channels
Web, Mobile App, Messaging
Platform
Ripplesk SaaS
🔍 The Challenge

A growing learner community with diverse, high-volume support needs

Mock Market serves a user base focused on learning, practicing, and competing in a simulated trading environment. This type of platform naturally generates a wide range of support needs, from onboarding and account questions to competition rules, rewards, rankings, app usage, and educational guidance. As the user base grows, those questions can quickly become difficult to manage across disconnected channels.

💡 The Solution

Unified support embedded directly into the user journey

Ripplesk gives Mock Market a unified way to support learners and traders across customer-facing channels. Instead of treating support as an afterthought, Mock Market can embed Ripplesk directly into the user journey — inside the website, within apps, and across messaging touchpoints. This makes it easier for users to get help at the moment they need it.

AI support helps answer common questions around competitions, platform usage, trading simulations, and support articles. Because many questions in a learning platform are repetitive, Ripplesk reduces pressure on human agents by handling those inquiries instantly and accurately. When a user needs more personalized help, the handover to a live agent remains smooth and contextual.

For a fast-growing simulation platform, simplicity is also essential. Ripplesk makes it easier to onboard support agents, maintain a consistent customer experience, and manage multiple communication channels from one dashboard. This gives Mock Market a more professional and scalable support structure without requiring a heavy support operations buildout.

📊 Impact with Ripplesk

Professional, scalable support without the overhead

Delivered support across web, mobile, and messaging channels in one place
Helped users get answers faster during competitions and active market events
Reduced repetitive workload through AI-powered support
Improved agent efficiency with a single dashboard and clear handover flow
Supported platform growth without increasing support complexity at the same rate
