Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?
Yes — every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features during your trial period.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise plans can also pay by bank transfer or invoice.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on monthly plans. If you're not happy with Ripplesk for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
How does per-agent pricing work?
Starter includes 2 agents. Pro includes unlimited agents. You'll never be charged per seat on Pro — add as many team members as you need.
